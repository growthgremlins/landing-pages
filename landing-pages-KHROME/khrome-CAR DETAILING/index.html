OUR SERVICES

Exterior Wash & Decontamination

Multi-stage hand wash removes all dirt, grime, and bonded contaminants. Clay bar treatment ensures your paint is clean down to the surface—ready for protection or just looking its best.

Paint Correction & Polishing

Machine polishing removes swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation to restore your paint’s original gloss. Brings back depth and clarity that washing alone can’t achieve.

Ceramic Coating & Protection

Nano-ceramic coating shields your paint from water, dirt, and UV damage — 6 month up to 10 year protections available. Hydrophobic protection makes maintenance effortless and keeps your car looking freshly detailed.

Headlight Restoration

Restore cloudy, yellowed headlights to crystal clarity through sanding, polishing, and UV sealing. Safer night driving and a better-looking vehicle in one service.

Deep Interior Cleaning & Detailing

Complete interior refresh with vacuum, steam cleaning, and every surface detailed. Dashboard, console, cup holders, vents, windows, and door panels—all spotless.

Leather Care & Conditioning

Professional leather cleaning and conditioning restores moisture, prevents cracking, and enhances color. UV protection keeps your seats supple and rich-looking year after year.

Carpet & Upholstery Shampooing

Hot water extraction lifts deep stains, odors, and allergens from carpets and seats. Pet stains, food spills, and ground-in dirt removed at the source.

Odor Removal & Sanitization

Eliminate smoke, pet, mildew, and food odors with ozone treatment and professional sanitizers. Kills bacteria at the source and leaves your cabin smelling fresh.
